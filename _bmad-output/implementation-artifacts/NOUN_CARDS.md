## Usage Instructions

- **Draw Cards**: Randomly select nouns to fill in the blanks in sentence cards
- **Combinations**: The mix of concrete (Pizza, Cat) and abstract (Dream, Magic) nouns creates interesting variety
- **Replayability**: Same sentence + different nouns = different prompt interpretation
- **Challenge**: More abstract nouns (151-200) create greater interpretation challenges
- **Scalability**: Can be expanded or customized for different game difficulty levels

## Examples of Filled-In Sentences

- Sentence: "The _______ was wearing sunglasses at a beach party"
  - Noun Card 2 (Robot): "The Robot was wearing sunglasses at a beach party"
  - Noun Card 180 (Soul): "The Soul was wearing sunglasses at a beach party"
  
- Sentence: "A _______ fell in love with a _______"
  - Noun Cards 123 & 150 (Pirate & Superhero): "A Pirate fell in love with a Superhero"
  - Noun Cards 95 & 7 (Tree & Ghost): "A Tree fell in love with a Ghost"

Mix and match for endless creative combinations!
