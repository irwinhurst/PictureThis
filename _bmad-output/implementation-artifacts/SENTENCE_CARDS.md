## Game Implementation Notes

- **Total Cards**: 200 unique sentence prompts with fill-in-the-blank format
- **Blank Distribution**: 
  - Cards 1-50: Mostly 1 blank, some with 2
  - Cards 51-100: Mix of 1-2 blanks
  - Cards 101-150: Mix of 1-2 blanks
  - Cards 151-200: 2-3 blanks (more challenging)
- **Gameplay**: Players select nouns to complete each sentence before illustrating
- **Difficulty**: Progressive difficulty from Cards 1-200
- **Scalability**: Can be expanded with additional card sets as needed

These fill-in-the-blank cards encourage creative noun selection and lead to unique, imaginative illustrations!
